----------  HOW TO PLAY  ------------------

For player 1:

Press 'W' to move the paddle up
Press 'S' to move the paddle down



For player 2:

Press "UP ARROW KEY" to move the paddle up
Press "DOWN ARROW KEY" to move the paddle down
